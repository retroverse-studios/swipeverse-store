SWIPEVERSE STORE

The community catalog for SwipeVerse — curated realities and story decks, reviewed before publish. Free, no accounts; everything you add plays offline in your browser.

▶ Play SwipeVerse Creator Guide

Browse in the app

The store lives inside SwipeVerse — open the game and tap Store to browse, add realities and stories to your collection, and manage your library.

Create & submit

Build decks in the in-game editor, check them with the Analyze tool, and submit via pull request. Educators: direct ?play= links for your class, no installs.

The catalog (raw)

Plain JSON, PR-curated, served as static files: catalog/decks.json and catalog/realities.json. What the app fetches is exactly what you see.

Art gallery

26 themed pixel-art sets for community decks — 22 hosted here (9 archetype scenes, card back, stat badges each) plus the 4 built into the game. Browse them all with suggested story genres, or pick straight from the deck builder.
